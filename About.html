Hi there!

I'm Ying Tian.
A UX researcher and designer thriving in Boston's vibrant culture. 

     

My journey

My journey in UX began with a background in Applied Psychology, which sparked my interest in understanding how people think and interact with technology. Since then, I've gained valuable experience working on a variety of projects, from designing features for smartphones at ZTE to overseeing UX enhancements for educational products at ALO7 that impacted millions of users.

Most recently, I completed the UX Design Fellow program at Springboard, further honing my skills in user research, design thinking, and usability testing. I'm a big believer in the power of data and use tools like Figma, Sketch, and R Studio to create effective designs based on user insights.

What excites me most about UX?

It's the process of uncovering user needs and pain points, and then using that knowledge to craft solutions that are not only beautiful but also truly useful. I love collaborating with teams to bridge the gap between user needs and business goals, and seeing the positive impact well-designed experiences can have.
